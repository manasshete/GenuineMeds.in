About Us

Our Mission

At GenuineMeds, we believe that everyone deserves access to clear, accurate, and reliable information about the medications they take. Our mission is to bridge the gap between complex medical terminology and everyday understanding.

Founded by a team of healthcare professionals and educators, GenuineMeds provides verified medical information in simple language that students, patients, and the general public can easily comprehend.

We collaborate with leading pharmacologists, physicians, and medical researchers to ensure that all information on our platform is up-to-date, accurate, and trustworthy.

Drug Information

Paracetamol

Used for pain relief and reducing high temperature. Common for headaches, toothaches, and cold symptoms.

Amoxicillin

An antibiotic used to treat a number of bacterial infections including ear infections and pneumonia.

Ibuprofen

A nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug used for treating pain, fever, and inflammation.

Omeprazole

Used to treat certain stomach and esophagus problems such as acid reflux and ulcers.
